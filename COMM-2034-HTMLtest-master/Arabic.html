ARABIC

Overview

Anyone who has a major under the Department of Political Science at Virginia Tech is required to have a language minor. Since my second major is international relations, I decided to minor in Arabic. Taking Arabic 1106 with Professor (or Ustadha) Nassereddine has definitely been a ride. She expects a lot from her students, and it's quite different than the first Elementary Arabic class with Dr. Azzam. This class is extremely fast-paced, and if you don't do your homework, you will be left behind.
